# Offtaker Partner One-Pager: Dedicated Power and Modular Energy Supply

Audience: Data center developers, industrial power users, industrial parks, mining, manufacturing, cold chain, logistics, and other large loads.

The company is an AI-native energy infrastructure developer. The current proof point is a concierge development screen used to evaluate dedicated power and modular supply opportunities near load.

## The Power Problem

Large loads increasingly need reliable, cost-aware, and deployable power options. Grid interconnection delays, utility constraints, and power price uncertainty can slow site development or expansion.

## What We Screen

- Dedicated power opportunities.
- Behind-the-meter power.
- Gas-to-power.
- Modular power supply.
- Stranded gas near load.
- Industrial energy supply options.
- Data center power site concepts.
- Reliability and redundancy concepts at screening level.

## How The Workflow Helps Offtakers

The workflow evaluates whether a resource, site, and commercial structure are credible enough for deeper work. It can compare power supply options, flag risks, identify missing data, and define next steps before an offtaker commits significant engineering or commercial resources.

## Potential Structures

- Behind-the-meter power.
- Dedicated generation.
- Tolling or energy services agreement.
- Power purchase agreement.
- Joint development with an operator or landowner.
- Industrial park energy supply.
- Hybrid modular power and storage configuration.

## Data Needed From Offtakers

- Load size and profile.
- Required reliability and redundancy.
- Target power price or budget range.
- Site location and timing.
- Interconnection status.
- Backup power requirements.
- Operating constraints.
- Preferred contract structure.

## Example Use Cases

- Data center dedicated power.
- Manufacturing plant expansion.
- Industrial park power supply.

- Cold chain or logistics power.
- Gas-to-industrial power near stranded resource.
