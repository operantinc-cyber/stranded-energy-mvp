# EPC and Vendor Partner One-Pager: Modular Energy Infrastructure Pipeline

Audience: EPC firms, modular facility providers, gas engine/turbine vendors, gas processing vendors, water treatment vendors, battery vendors.

The company is an AI-native energy infrastructure developer. The current proof point is a concierge development screen used to package practical projects and identify where vendor input is needed.

## Partner Opportunity

The Stranded Energy Development Screen creates a structured way to identify and package early modular infrastructure opportunities before they are ready for detailed engineering or procurement.

## Why The MVP Matters

Many stranded gas, flare gas, marginal field, and modular power opportunities need early constructability input and budgetary ranges before they can move forward. The MVP helps qualify which opportunities are worth vendor attention and what information is missing.

## Where Vendors Fit In The Workflow

- Provide budgetary quotes.
- Suggest modular configurations.
- Review constructability constraints.
- Help define equipment blocks and lead times.
- Flag technical risks early.
- Support vendor shortlist development.
- Contribute to repeatable project templates.

## What We Need From Vendors

- Typical equipment ranges and constraints.
- Budgetary cost ranges.
- Lead time assumptions.
- Site requirements.
- Gas quality or feedstock limits.
- Utility, water, emissions, or footprint requirements.
- Preferred data package for early quoting.

## What Vendors Get

- Early access to qualified opportunities.
- Better-scoped requests before formal quotes.
- Visibility into repeatable project types.
- Potential inclusion in vendor shortlists.
- Partner feedback loop on practical modular configurations.

## Example Project Types

- Stranded gas-to-power.
- Flare gas power.
- Modular EPF or marginal field redevelopment.
- Gas processing and NGL recovery.
- Compression and fuel conditioning.
- Water treatment for field redevelopment.
- Battery or hybrid power systems.
