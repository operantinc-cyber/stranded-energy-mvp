# Pricing Sheet: Stranded Energy Development Screen

These are early commercial ranges for selling the Stranded Energy Development Screen as a concierge MVP service from an AI-native energy infrastructure developer. Final pricing should reflect project complexity, data quality, urgency, jurisdiction, and third-party support needs.

## Package 1: Opportunity Diagnostic

- Price range: $1,500-$5,000
- Timeline: 3-5 business days
- Best for: quick go/no-go review

Deliverables:

- Intake review
- High-level opportunity summary
- Data gap list
- Initial monetization options
- Preliminary go/no-go view

## Package 2: Development Screen

- Price range: $10,000-$35,000
- Timeline: 10-15 business days
- Best for: serious opportunities needing structured screening

Deliverables:

- Full project development memo
- Gas-to-power or modular infrastructure sizing
- Preliminary economics
- Risk register
- Monetization pathway ranking
- Recommended development concept
- Next-step roadmap

## Package 3: Advanced Pre-Feasibility Support

- Price range: $50,000-$150,000+
- Timeline: 4-8 weeks
- Best for: opportunities moving toward partner, investor, or offtaker discussions

Deliverables:

- Deeper engineering review
- Vendor budgetary input coordination
- Permitting pathway review
- Refined financial model
- Offtaker/counterparty strategy
- Investor or partner memo support

## Package 4: Development Partnership

- Price range: Custom
- Timeline: Project-specific
- Best for: projects where the founder/company helps originate, structure, finance, or develop the project as a development partner

Commercial structures:

- Development fee
- Success fee
- Project equity
- Carried interest
- Revenue share
- O&M or optimization fee

## Pricing Notes And Assumptions

- Prices are preliminary and may be adjusted based on project complexity.
- Travel, third-party engineering, legal, environmental, permitting, vendor, or market data costs are excluded unless specifically included.
- Emerging market or high-risk jurisdiction work may require additional scope.
- Work begins after signed engagement letter and NDA where needed.

## When To Choose Each Package

- Choose Opportunity Diagnostic when the client needs a fast first-pass view before sharing deeper data or committing budget.
- Choose Development Screen when there is a credible opportunity that needs a structured memo, economics, risks, and recommendation.
- Choose Advanced Pre-Feasibility Support when the opportunity is moving toward investor, partner, offtaker, vendor, or board-level discussions.
- Choose Development Partnership when the founder/company is expected to help originate, structure, finance, or participate in the project beyond analysis.
