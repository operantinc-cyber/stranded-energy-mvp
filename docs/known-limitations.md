# Known Limitations

This internal development desk is a preliminary local screening tool for an AI-native energy infrastructure developer. It is intentionally simple.

## Data Quality Limitations

- Outputs are only as good as the data entered.
- Seed data is illustrative.
- Missing fields may cause the app to rely on defaults or conservative assumptions.
- The app does not verify operator data, production data, gas analysis, ownership, contracts, permits, or costs.

## Screening-Level Calculations

- Scoring, gas-to-power, financial, risk, and monetization outputs are rule-based.
- Calculations are directional and intended for early triage.
- Results should not be treated as final development conclusions.

## No Live GIS

- No full GIS layer system.
- No satellite imagery.
- No pipeline GIS layers.
- No road, substation, fiber, load, or data center geospatial validation.

## No Live Market Data

- No live power prices.
- No gas prices.
- No capacity prices.
- No congestion data.
- No basis differentials.
- No commodity price forecasts.

## No Interconnection Modeling

- No utility queue data.
- No load flow analysis.
- No upgrade cost estimate.
- No export constraint analysis.
- No interconnection schedule validation.

## No Engineering Drawings

- No site layout.
- No single-line diagram.
- No PFD or P&ID.
- No equipment specification.
- No gas treatment design.
- No EPC package.

## No Legal Or Regulatory Determination

- No land title review.
- No mineral rights opinion.
- No gas rights opinion.
- No permit determination.
- No environmental legal opinion.
- No contract review.

## No Bankable Financial Model

- No debt model.
- No tax model.
- No depreciation.
- No working capital.
- No construction draw schedule.
- No IRR or NPV.
- No lender-grade sensitivities.

## No Automated External Data Ingestion

- No external APIs.
- No document parsing.
- No live database feeds.
- No automatic market, GIS, permit, or counterparty data ingestion.
