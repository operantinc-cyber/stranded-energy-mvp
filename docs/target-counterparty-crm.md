# Target Counterparty CRM Template

This is a lightweight business development tracker for the Stranded Energy Development Screen MVP. It supports the internal development desk of an AI-native energy infrastructure developer. Use it to organize outreach to operators, investors, EPC partners, vendors, offtakers, lawyers, permitting advisors, and data center or industrial power contacts during market testing.

The goal is not to build a full CRM. The goal is to maintain a practical target list, track next actions, and identify qualified opportunities that should move into the client intake workflow.

## Relevance Scoring Guidance

- 1 = low relevance
- 2 = weak fit
- 3 = possible fit
- 4 = strong fit
- 5 = priority target

Use relevance score to decide where founder time should go first. A high score should usually mean the contact owns assets, controls capital, can validate a technical pathway, can introduce credible opportunities, or can become a repeat partner.

## Relationship Strength Guidance

- Cold
- Warm
- Existing relationship
- Strategic relationship

Relationship strength should reflect actual access and trust, not just target importance. A cold priority target may still be important, but it likely needs a warm introduction or more careful outreach.

## Practical Use

1. Start with 100 targets across the recommended categories.
2. Score each target from 1 to 5 for relevance.
3. Prioritize warm or existing relationships with relevance scores of 4 or 5.
4. Track one clear next action for every active contact.
5. Move qualified opportunities into the client intake process before creating an opportunity in the app.
