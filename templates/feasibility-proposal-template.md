## Scope Of Work

The proposed scope includes:

- Intake review and data gap assessment.
- Review of available resource, infrastructure, commercial, and regulatory information.
- Screening-level gas-to-power or modular infrastructure sizing, as applicable.
- Preliminary financial screen using available assumptions.
- Risk register development.
- Monetization pathway comparison.
- Recommended development concept.
- Go/no-go recommendation and next-step roadmap.

## Deliverables

- Project development memo.
- Preliminary sizing and economics summary.
- Risk register.
- Monetization options comparison.
- Data gap and follow-up request list.
- Decision meeting to review findings and next steps.

## Client Responsibilities

- Infrastructure information.

- Available maps, drawings, permits, contracts, photos, and studies.

## Exclusions

This proposal excludes:

- Final engineering design.
- Legal advice.
- Environmental opinion.
- Reserve certification.
- Interconnection study.
- Bankable feasibility study.
- Third-party engineering, legal, environmental, permitting, vendor, travel, or market data costs unless specifically included.
